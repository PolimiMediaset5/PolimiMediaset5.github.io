Mina El Hammani
CO-FOUNDER & CTO
Professional highliner, rigger, extreme sports lover. I love this niche where I can share my passions: documentaries, traveling and extreme sports.
I believe in the power of narrative stories as performance objects in society, documentaries differ from blockbuster films in the way the stories are unfolded on people, the key elements of our goals relies on the people themselves, we don’t invent anything new but rather share it. I do believe in the power of technology as a tool rather than a mean, I’ve been always interested on new ways to exploit the “wow” effect on my films , something I hope stick to them in a reflective way.
